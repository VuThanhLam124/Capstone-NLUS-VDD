Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make a voice query?

Click the microphone button on the Voice Query page and speak your question naturally. For example, "Show me total sales for last month" or "What are my top 5 customers by revenue?"

Can I save my queries for later?

Yes! After running a query, click the bookmark icon to save it. You can access all your saved queries from the "Saved Queries" section in the sidebar.

What file formats can I export?

VoiceQuery AI supports exporting data in CSV, Excel (.xlsx), JSON, and PDF formats. You can choose your preferred format in the Export Data section.

How accurate is the voice recognition?

Our AI-powered voice recognition has a 98%+ accuracy rate for common business queries. If the system doesn't understand your query, you can rephrase or type it directly.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption (AES-256) and all data is processed securely. Voice recordings are not stored after processing, and your business data remains private.
